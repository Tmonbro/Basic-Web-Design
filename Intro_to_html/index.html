Who is Naruto Uzumaki

Naruto Uzumaki is an orphan child that grew up to become the leader of his village. As a kid he was treated as a black sheep due to the fact he possess a specail power. A power even he did not know he had. He was, what they called, the nine tails Jinchuriki.

How did he become a Jinchuriki?

In the world of Naruto, a Jinchuriki, meaning ("human pillar of power") is an individual who has a tailed beast sealed inside of them, granting them access to the beast's power. Tailed beastare powerful, chakra-based beings with animalistic forms, each possessing a different number of tails unique abilities. The more about of tails the stronger the beast is. There are a total of nine individual tails beast, but they can combine together and make what they the "Ten Tails"
